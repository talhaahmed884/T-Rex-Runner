                                Instructions
Controls:                                         

	1. UP, DOWN, LEFT and RIGHT keys can be pressed to move the Dino on to
	   the screen in these positions respectively.

	2. SpaceBar key can be pressed to jump the Dino off the hurdle.

	3. Escape Key can be pressed to pause the game.

Game Play:

	1. As the dino moves over the hurdles, the score is added up
	   accordingly.
	
	2. If the score crosses 50 points each time, the level and
	   speed of Dino is increased.
	
	3. A player has total three lives in the game. If all the
	   lives are destroyed then game ends.

	4. Health boost might appear after certain score or
	   increase in level.
